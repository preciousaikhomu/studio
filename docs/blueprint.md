# **App Name**: Hameedah Haven

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display a visually appealing hero section with brand introduction and high-quality product imagery.
- Product Catalog: Organize products into categories: Adire, Perfumes & Body Sprays, Drinks (Zobo & Tigernut Milk), and Household Essentials, with clean product cards including images and names.
- Brand Story: An About page presenting the brand story and identity, emphasizing quality and authenticity.
- Contact Information: A contact page providing phone, WhatsApp, and social media contact details, with a contact form layout.
- WhatsApp Chat Integration: Integrate a WhatsApp chat button for direct customer communication.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft terracotta (#E07A5F) to evoke warmth and earthiness, aligning with natural fabrics and ingredients.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), a heavily desaturated version of the primary color, for a clean and inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Dusty rose (#D87093), analogous to terracotta but with higher saturation and brightness, highlighting calls to action and important information.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif that suits the aesthetic of fashion and art.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', a serif font which will create a pleasant reading experience.
- Ensure a smooth, responsive layout with clear sections, adapting to various screen sizes for optimal mobile viewing.
- Incorporate simple, elegant icons for product categories and navigation to enhance usability.
